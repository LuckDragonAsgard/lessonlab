Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 May 2026  |  Luck Dragon Pty Ltd ABN 64 697 434 898

1. Who we are

LessonLab is operated by Luck Dragon Pty Ltd ("we", "us", "our"), an Australian company. Contact us at hello@luckdragon.io.

2. Information we collect

Account data: email address, display name, school name, and password (stored as a salted hash — we never store your plain-text password).

Usage data: lesson plans you generate and save, monthly generation counts, and lesson ratings you submit.

Payment data: billing is processed by Stripe. We do not store card numbers or banking details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.

Technical data: standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) retained for up to 90 days for security purposes.

3. How we use your information

We use your information to: provide and improve the Service; process payments; send transactional emails (account confirmations, password resets); and monitor for abuse. We do not sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising.

4. AI processing

Lesson generation requests (subject, year level, unit, focus) are sent to Anthropic's API to generate lesson content. We do not send personally identifiable information (name, email) to Anthropic. Anthropic's usage policies apply. Generated lessons are stored in our database associated with your account.

5. Data storage

Your data is stored in Cloudflare's D1 database infrastructure. Cloudflare operates data centres globally; data may be processed in multiple regions. Cloudflare's data processing agreement applies.

6. Data retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing hello@luckdragon.io. We will action deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Sharing

We share data only with: Anthropic (lesson generation, no PII); Stripe (billing); Resend (transactional email); and Cloudflare (hosting and database). All are subject to their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

8. Your rights (Australian Privacy Act 1988)

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information we hold about you. Contact us at hello@luckdragon.io to exercise these rights.

9. Cookies

LessonLab uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to store your session token locally on your device. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

10. Children

LessonLab is designed for use by teachers (adults). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. Student data (lesson content about student groups) does not include individual student names or identifiers.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated by email. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance.
