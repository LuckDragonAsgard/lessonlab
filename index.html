ELEMENT 2 — ENABLING LEARNING
Learning Intention: We are learning to run with control in shared space and stop safely on a signal.
Vocabulary (3 tiers)
• Tier 1: run, stop, walk, listen
• Tier 2: freeze, signal, boundary
• Tier 3: 3-step stop, spatial awareness
Self-regulation prompts
• "What helps you remember 3-step stop?"
• "How will you know you’re safe?"

Chunk 2 (3m): Unsafe demo — "what was wrong?"
Chunk 3 (4m): Teach 3-step stop — plant · bend · small step.
Sentence stems
