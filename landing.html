Week 4 — Persuasive Writing: Developing an Argument
Learning Intention
I can identify and write a clear argument supported by at least two reasons.
Success Criteria
  • I can state my opinion clearly in a topic sentence
  • I can write two reasons that support my argument
  • I can use persuasive language (because, therefore, clearly)
Equipment
Mentor texts, persuasive language word wall, lined writing paper, graphic organiser
Teaching Phases
Warm-up 10 min Teach 15 min Apply 20 min Review 10 min Close 5 min
