Refund Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

7-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with LessonLab within your first 7 days, email us at hello@lessonlab.com.au and we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.

Monthly subscriptions

  • You may cancel anytime from your account settings.
  • On cancellation, your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
  • After the first 7 days, we do not offer pro-rata refunds for the unused portion of a monthly billing period.

Annual subscriptions

  • Annual subscriptions are eligible for a full refund within 7 days of purchase.
  • After 7 days, annual subscriptions are non-refundable except where required by Australian Consumer Law (see below).
  • If you cancel an annual plan early, your access continues until the end of the annual period — no partial refund is issued.

Billing errors

If you were charged incorrectly (duplicate charge, wrong amount, charge after cancellation), contact us immediately at hello@lessonlab.com.au. We will investigate and issue a full refund for any confirmed billing error within 5 business days.

Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this policy limits your rights under the Australian Consumer Law (ACL). If LessonLab has a major failure — meaning the service does not do what it was supposed to do and the problem cannot be fixed — you are entitled to a refund under the ACL regardless of when you purchased.

How to request a refund

Email hello@lessonlab.com.au with:

  • The email address on your account.
  • Your reason for requesting a refund (optional but helpful).

We process all eligible refunds within 5 business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method via Stripe.
